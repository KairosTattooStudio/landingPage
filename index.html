KAIROS

Tattoo Studio

Generations of Exceptional Tattooing

Kairos Studio is a private body art space built on precision, discipline, and timeless aesthetics. Inspired by classical sculpture and blackwork design, each piece is created with intention and technical excellence.


Operating with 100% pre-sterilized disposable equipment and strict biosecurity protocols, Kairos combines art and clinical-level hygiene in a clean, professional environment.


Timeless art. Precise execution. No shortcuts.

Ihsel Hinojosa is part of the resident artist team, bringing his vision and technique to every piece.
